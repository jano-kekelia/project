About Judo

Judo is a modern martial art created by Jigoro Kano. It focuses on throws, joint locks, and pins, emphasizing balance, flexibility, and technique.

About Muay Thai

Muay Thai is an ancient Thai martial art known as the "Art of Eight Limbs." It incorporates punches, elbows, knees, and shins, with an emphasis on striking and clinch work.
