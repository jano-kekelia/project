Wrestling

Wrestling is a combat sport where two individuals fight to gain control over each other using various techniques. Here are some of the most common styles:

  • Freestyle Wrestling
  • Greco-Roman Wrestling
  • Submission Wrestling
  • Professional Wrestling

Famous wrestlers include names like The Rock, John Cena, and Stone Cold Steve Austin.

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport where two competitors fight using only their fists, aiming to knock out their opponent or score more points through punches. The sport has a rich history with global champions.

  • Heavyweight
  • Lightweight
  • Middleweight
  • Welterweight

Some legendary boxers include Muhammad Ali, Mike Tyson, and Floyd Mayweather.
